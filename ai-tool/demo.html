Analysis Examples

Our AI detection system evaluates multiple factors to determine if a message is legitimate or phishing:

  • Linguistic Patterns: Urgency language, grammatical errors, threatening tones
  • URL Analysis: Domain age, suspicious TLDs, misspelled brand names
  • Sender Verification: Email header analysis, domain alignment
  • Social Engineering: False urgency, authority claims, reward promises

The final risk score combines all these factors, weighted by their predictive importance.
