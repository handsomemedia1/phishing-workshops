Workshop Series Overview

A comprehensive program to build phishing detection skills with AI assistance

Workshop Progression

Our cybersecurity sensitization program consists of 5 progressive workshops designed to build students' phishing detection skills through hands-on interaction with our AI tool.

1

Introduction

Phishing fundamentals and AI tool demonstration

2

Pattern Recognition

Analysis of linguistic patterns in phishing messages

3

Technical Analysis

URL examination and behavioral cues in attacks

4

Practical Application

Hands-on lab with real-world examples

5

Assessment

Performance review and certification

Learning Objectives

By completing the full workshop series, students will:

  • Identify common linguistic patterns used in phishing attempts
  • Analyze URLs for suspicious characteristics
  • Recognize social engineering and behavioral manipulation tactics
  • Develop practical assessment skills using AI-assisted tools
  • Demonstrate improved phishing detection accuracy (compared to our AI's 94% benchmark)

AI Tool Integration

Our phishing detection AI with 94% accuracy is central to all workshop activities:

Workshop AI Tool Integration
Workshop 1 Introduction and demonstration of the tool's capabilities
Workshop 2 Using the tool to analyze linguistic patterns
Workshop 3 Applying the tool to URL and behavioral analysis
Workshop 4 Hands-on lab with full tool access
Workshop 5 Reviewing performance metrics and certification

Workshop Format

Each 90-minute workshop follows a consistent structure:

10 min

Introduction

Review of previous material and introduction of new concepts

15 min

Demonstration

Instructor-led examples using the AI tool

30 min

Interactive Exercise

Hands-on student activities with the AI tool

20 min

Group Discussion

Analysis of findings and practical applications

15 min

Assessment

Brief quiz or exercise to measure understanding
