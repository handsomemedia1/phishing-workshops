Workshop 1: Introduction to Phishing & AI Tools

The foundation of phishing awareness and AI-powered detection

Workshop Overview

This initial workshop introduces students to the fundamentals of phishing threats and demonstrates our AI tool's capabilities in detecting malicious communications.

90 minutes
No prerequisites
Beginner Level

Learning Objectives

By the end of this workshop, students will be able to:

  • Define phishing and explain its common variations
  • Identify the primary motivations behind phishing attacks
  • Understand how AI tools can assist in phishing detection
  • Recognize basic indicators of phishing attempts

Workshop Agenda

1. Introduction to Phishing (25 min)

  • Definition and history of phishing
  • Types of phishing (spear phishing, whaling, smishing, etc.)
  • Recent statistics and case studies
  • Why phishing remains effective despite awareness

2. AI Tool Demonstration (20 min)

  • Introduction to our AI detection system (94% accuracy)
  • Live demonstration analyzing known phishing examples
  • Explanation of key indicators the AI identifies
  • How machine learning improves detection capabilities

3. Interactive Exercise: "Spot the Phish" (25 min)

  • Students review sample emails without AI assistance
  • Group discussion of suspicious elements
  • Run samples through the AI tool
  • Compare human vs. AI detection accuracy

4. Group Discussion (15 min)

  • Why do phishing attacks trick people?
  • Psychological tactics used by attackers
  • Common misconceptions about phishing
  • How AI can complement human judgment

5. Assessment & Conclusion (5 min)

  • Brief quiz to test understanding
  • Preview of Workshop 2: Linguistic Pattern Analysis
  • Resources for further learning

AI Tool Integration

During this workshop, students will:

  • Observe the AI tool's 94% accuracy on test datasets
  • See how the tool highlights suspicious elements in emails
  • Understand the basic principles behind AI-powered detection
  • Learn how to access and use the tool interface
